### ✨ Why this app is different?
Forget the boring timers! My Pomodoro app turns your study sessions into an exciting adventure:

* **Earn Rewards:** Every time you finish a study set, you earn coins! Yayyy! ٩(♡ε♡)۶
* **Customize Your Style:** Use your hard-earned coins to purchase adorable outfits for your character. 👗✨
* **Decorate Your Space:** Buy cute furniture and decorations to design your character's dream room. 🎀🧸

Stay productive, stay motivated, and have fun building your own little pixel world! (o^▽^o)
